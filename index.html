Welcome To Our Home Stay Resort

Welcome to our Krutharth Nivas (A Beachside Home Stay), where paradise meets comfort! Nestled along the shimmering coastline, our charming retreat offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Immerse yourself in the soothing sound of crashing waves, feel the warm sand beneath your toes, and let the refreshing sea breeze sweep away your worries.

Our beachside home stay is designed to provide you with a serene and rejuvenating experience. Each accommodation is thoughtfully crafted to blend modern amenities with coastal aesthetics, creating a perfect blend of luxury and relaxation. Wake up to breathtaking views of the ocean from your private balcony or terrace, and fall asleep to the gentle lullaby of the tides.
